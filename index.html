PINA COLADA

Ingredients

  • 🍍 Pineapple juice – 1 cup (fresh or canned)
  • 🧊 Ice cubes – 1 to 1½ cups
  • 🍹 White rum – 1/2 cup (optional for non-alcoholic version)
  • 🥥 Coconut cream – 1/2 cup (like Coco Lopez or any thick coconut cream)
  • 🍍 Pineapple slice – for garnish (optional)

Recepie

  1. Combine pineapple juice, coconut cream, ice, and rum (if using) in a blender.
  2. Blend for about 30–45 seconds until the mixture is creamy and frothy.
  3. If it’s too thick, add a splash more juice. For extra sweetness, add a little honey or sugar.
  4. Use a hurricane glass or any tall glass of your choice.
  5. Top with a pineapple slice and a cherry. Serve immediately with a straw!

Blue Lagoon

Ingredients

  • 💙 Blue Curaçao syrup – 2 tbsp (non-alcoholic version available)
  • 🍋 Lemonade or Sprite – 1 cup
  • 🧊 Ice cubes – as needed
  • 🍋 Lemon slice and 🍒 cherry – for garnish (optional)

Recepie

  1. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  2. Pour Blue Curaçao syrup into the glass.
  3. Top it up with lemonade or Sprite.
  4. Garnish with a lemon slice and cherry if desired.
  5. Serve chilled with a straw!

Pink Paradise

Ingredients

  • 🍓 Strawberry juice – 1/2 cup (fresh or packaged)
  • 🥥 Coconut water – 1/2 cup (chilled)
  • 🍋 Lemon juice – 1 tbsp (fresh)
  • 🍯 Honey or sugar syrup – 1 tbsp (optional)
  • 🍒 Cherry or mint – for garnish

Recepie

  1. Add strawberry juice, coconut water, and lemon juice into a shaker or blender.
  2. Add honey/sugar if you want it sweeter.
  3. Shake or blend well with crushed ice.
  4. Pour into a tall chilled glass.
  5. Serve chilled with a straw!
